Events

At Alaska Awaits, we are connected with vendors and event coordinators throughout the entire state of Alaska. We have an event for you, from bustling Juneau city life to the remote fishing villages not found on any map. Alaska Awaits has researched for you and filled out the calendar with events year-round with something happening at least once a month. If your vacation is in the middle of winter, we will still have an event for you sponsored by us or one of our many partners.

Train Tour

Our fully inclusive train tour is a hidden gem if taking the scenic route interests you. Too busy in your day-to-day? Now is the time to take things easy and enjoy a fully catered experience; all meals come from a five-star rated chef utilizing locally sourced ingredients for guaranteed freshness. It is not uncommon for the fish on the menu to have been caught just hours before it hits your plate.

Low Inclusive Pricing

$949.99

Per-person includes four nights of hotel, room service, all meals, all drinks including adult, local flight from ANC airport, and all ground travel.

Train Tour

Train Tours are available year-round, starting on Tuesdays, Fridays, and Sundays. The tour is three nights on the train and includes four nights of hotel accommodations to eliminate any rush and allow for ground travel and exploration.
