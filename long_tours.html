What Does Intensity Mean?

Low Intensity:

Low intensity is done at a comfortable pace. Here are clues that your exercise intensity is at a low level:

  • Your heart rate should stay steady at about 50% of its maximum rate.
  • This steady pace should last for periods of at least 30 minutes.
  • You should be able to carry on a conversation without getting breathless.

Medium Intensity:

Medium intensity feels somewhat hard. Here are clues that your exercise intensity is at a medium level:

  • Your breathing quickens, but you're not out of breath.
  • You start to lightly sweat after about 10 minutes of activity.
  • You can talk to someone, but you can't sing.

High Intensity:

High intensity feels challenging. Here are clues that your exercise intensity is at a high level:

  • Your breathing is deep and rapid.
  • You start to sweat after only a few minutes of activity.
  • You can't say more than a few words without pausing for breath.
