Features of Alaska Awaits


Trails and Forest Info:

  • With nearly one thousand trails and thousands of miles to explore, Alaska has trails for novice to advanced hikers as well as motor vehicles.
  • Alaska and its trails offer unique and different experiences between its winter and summer seasons. Consider this when planning your tour with Alaska Awaits!
  • Trails are a fantastic way to experience some of the iconic sites in Alaska including the Alaska Range mountains, the Tongass National Forest, and their many alpine lakes, rivers, and streams along the trails.
  • Wether on a guided hike or a selfguided one, encounters with animals are common and the wilds of Alaska are home to many variety of wildlife.

Animals of Alaska:

  • Bear (grizzly, brown, black, polar)
  • Moose
  • Caribou
  • Wolf
  • Eagles
  • Whales (humpback and gray)
  • Orcas
  • And many more.

Water Info:

  • Alaska is home to and known for its five native varieties of salmon and is the primary provider of sustainable salmon in the U.S..
  • Those sustainable fishing practices provide excellent angling opportunities that are renound globally.
  • Surrounding Oceans make for majestic whale watching cruises with glaciers in the background.
  • Want to experience the majesty for yourself? Alaska Awaits Tours provide the ultimate outdoor excursion for you, your friends, and family.

There are many bodies of water in Alaska, some of which are alpine. It is also surrounded by both the Arctic and Pacific Oceans bringing opportunities for world-class fishing and whale watching.
